About Brush & Pixel

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Brush & Pixel was born from a passion for connecting talented artists with art lovers worldwide.

We believe that art is essential to human expression and that everyone should have access to unique, original pieces that speak to them. Our curated collection represents diverse styles, mediums, and perspectives, ensuring there's something for every taste and space.

Our team of art experts carefully selects each piece in our collection, focusing on quality, originality, and artistic vision. We work directly with artists to ensure fair compensation and recognition for their talent.
