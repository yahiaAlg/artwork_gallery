About the Artwork

"Seaside Dreams" was inspired by the artist's childhood summers spent on the coast of Maine. The painting captures not just the visual beauty of the ocean, but the feelings of peace and nostalgia that come with seaside memories.

Parker uses a technique of layered oil paints to create depth and movement in the water, while the subtle color palette of blues, greens, and golden hues evokes the particular quality of light found near the ocean during the golden hour.

The horizon line, positioned using the golden ratio, draws the viewer's eye across the canvas and creates a sense of expansiveness that is both calming and uplifting.
